Demographics

Glendale Arizona has a population of about 246,000 people, the median age is 33 years old with a median household income of $60,499. The 5 largest ethnic groups in Glendale, AZ are White (non-hispanic) (43.6%), white (hispanic) (20.4%), hispanic (11.4%), other 7.42%.

Exploration and Outdoors

There is a pelothora of outdoor activities and areas to explore in Glendale AZ. North of Glendale is the Lake Pleasant area with shooting ranges, off-roading, rock crawling, lake activities, hiking and camping. In Glendale there is resturants, stores, malls, and stadium events like football. Enjoy the sunlight, and stay hydrated!

Economy

Glendale is consistently expanding. New businesses are formed daily, areas are being improved, roads repaired, and buildings being built for expansion. Not only is Glendale a great opportunity for new businesses, but also new families. Houses and apartments are built in bulk and we continue to build further west. Instead of expanding up, Glendale is expanding outward.
